With this flexible programme you gain credit from taking short courses and attending the Oxford University Summer School for Adults (OUSSA). The Department offers a range of part-time postgraduate study options in an array of subject areas. Certificates, diplomas, masters' degrees and DPhil (PhD) programmes are a great way to further your study, progress in your career or learn for personal enrichment. Programme formats and delivery vary but some are taught mostly online, while others are formed of online study with short residencies in Oxford.
